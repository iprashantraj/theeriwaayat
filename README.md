# Riwaayat Publications

> Where Every Voice Becomes a Legacy

Official website for **Riwaayat Publications** — a book publishing house specializing in solo releases, anthologies, and a growing community of writers across English and Hindi.

🌐 **Live:** [theeriwaayat.vercel.app](https://theeriwaayat.vercel.app)

---

## About

Riwaayat Publications began as **WePublish Publication** in March 2024 with a mission to democratize publishing. In April 2026, it evolved into **Riwaayat** — a premium identity dedicated to literary tradition, high-end formatting, and minimalist aesthetics.

The site showcases:

- The three publishing tracks (Solo Book, Paid Anthology, Free Anthology)
- Portfolio of published solo releases and curated anthologies
- The journey from WePublish to Riwaayat
- Community of 420+ writers and 30+ projects

## Stack

- **HTML5** · semantic markup, single-page scroll
- **CSS3** · mobile-first, custom properties, fluid typography (`clamp()`), grid + flexbox
- **Vanilla JavaScript** · IntersectionObserver-based scroll animations, animated stat counters, swipeable carousel, accordion sections
- **Google Fonts** · [Della Respira](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Della+Respira) (display), [Harmattan](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Harmattan) (body)
- **Hosting** · [Vercel](https://vercel.com) — static deployment, automatic CI/CD on push to `main`

No build step. No framework. No dependencies.
